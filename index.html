Our Vision - Donating to Humanitarian Causes Directly, Without Having to go Through Traditional Donation Channels

XoroCoin is designed to be transparent. That means every transaction is recorded on a public ledger, so you can see exactly where your donation is going. And it's decentralized, which means it's not controlled by any government or financial institution. This gives you more control over your donation and means there are no intermediaries involved, so more of your funds go directly to the cause.

Meet The Team

In February of 2023, six university students came together to create a new cryptocurrency with a charitable twist. The currency operates on a donation-based system, meaning that users can donate their coins to various charities and organizations that the group has partnered with. The students hope that their cryptocurrency will not only serve as a secure and efficient means of transaction but also encourage users to make a positive impact in their communities. With the rise of cryptocurrency, this innovative approach to fundraising has the potential to make a significant difference in the world of philanthropy.
