Our Vision

XoroCoin allows people to donate to humanitarian causes directly, without having to go through traditional donation channels. That way, you know your funds are going to the cause you care about.

Transparency

XoroCoin is designed to be transparent. That means every transaction is recorded on a public ledger, so you can see exactly where your donation is going.

Decentralization

XoroCoin is decentralized, which means it's not controlled by any government or financial institution. This gives you more control over your donation.

Meet the Team

On February of 2023, six university students came together to create a new cryptocurrency with a charitable twist. The students hope that their cryptocurrency will not only serve as a secure and efficient means of transaction but also encourage users to make a positive impact in their communities.
